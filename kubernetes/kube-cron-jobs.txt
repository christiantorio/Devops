A CronJob creates a watch loop which will create a batch job on your behalf when the 
time becomes true. We Will use our existing Job Ô¨Åle to start. {% endcomment %}

#creating a job

apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: Job
metadata:
  name: sleepy
spec:
  completions: 5 #how many times you want the job to be completed
  parallelism: 2 #two pods at a time will be deployed
  activeDeadlineSeconds: 15 #job and all pods will end one it runs for 15 seconds
  template:
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: resting
        image: busybox
        command: ["/bin/sleep"]
        args: ["5"]
      restartPolicy: Never


##Cron job
apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: CronJob
metadata:
  name: sleepy
spec:
  schedule: "/2 * * * *"
  jobTemplate:
    spec:
      template:
        spec:
          activeDeadlineSeconds: 10W
          containers:
          - name: resting
              image: busybox
              command: ["/bin/sleep"]
              args: ["30"]
          restartPolicy: Never
